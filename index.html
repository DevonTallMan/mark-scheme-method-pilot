EXAM QUESTION — 3 MARKS

An e-commerce company uses targeted marketing based on customer purchase history. Explain one way this adds value to the business. (3 Marks)

❌ TYPICAL STUDENT ANSWER 1 / 3

"Using data helps the business make better decisions about marketing. They can target the right customers and improve their sales. This is useful because it means they are more efficient and can make more money."

Examiner's note: 1 mark for mentioning targeted marketing. No marks for explanation — "make better decisions" has no mechanism. No marks for impact — "make more money" is too generic. This is the answer most students write.
✅ NEI METHOD ANSWER 3 / 3

"The company could use predictive modelling to analyse a customer's past purchase history and identify products they are statistically likely to buy next. This works by cross-referencing browsing behaviour with purchase frequency data to generate a personalised recommendation engine. This allows the business to increase average order value per customer, improving revenue without acquiring new customers and reducing the cost per conversion."

NAME — 1st mark
EXPLAIN — 2nd mark
IMPACT — 3rd mark (the one most students miss)
